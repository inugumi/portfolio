Cupcake Your Way

I redesigned the current website for Cupcake Your Way, a startup catering service for small and local events in the area. I conducted research and improved upon userflow and consistency. I produced sketches, wireframes, and mockups. The primary goal was to redesign and improve upon the existing site in order to draw more traffic.

This was a process that was taken from start to finish so that I could gain a better understanding of the general UX design process.

Sketches

After determining what could be improved upon on the original design, rapid sketches were created. Feedback was taken across multiple iterations until there was a clear direction with how the redesign should go. The original design proved too busy and too inconsistent with little to no clear direction on where the user should go next. Sketches were created with simplicity and clear direction in mind.

Wireframes

Because this was a startup, a clear and minimalistic design was the priority. The structure of the original website was redone to have more clarity and hierarchy. Navigation was more easily accessible and location was added to the navigation bar to provide a clear indication on where the service was being provided. A hero and call-to-action was provided, the contact section was updated, and testimonials and instagram gallery were added to bolster the site.

Mockups

Once there was a solid design direction, I produced high fidelity mockups of the website that could then be converted into prototypes. Some minor alterations were made along the way, including a change to the hero section to include a little more information and add more imagery. Gave the site a more consistent colour scheme, limiting the palette to 3 main colours.
